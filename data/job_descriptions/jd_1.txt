Job Title: Senior Key Account Manager - Global Sales

Location: Dallas, TX (On-site)

Company: Inash Oil & Petroleum

Type: Full-time

About the Role:
Inash Oil & Petroleum is a leading global energy provider seeking a high-performing, strategic Senior Key Account Manager to join our team in Dallas. This is a pivotal role responsible for driving significant revenue growth within our existing client portfolio while securing new, large-scale business opportunities. You will be at the forefront of our sales strategy, managing complex, multi-million dollar accounts and leading a team of sales professionals.

Key Responsibilities:

Revenue Growth: Develop and execute strategic sales plans to achieve and exceed ambitious annual sales goals.

Global RFP Management: Lead the response to global Request for Proposals (RFPs), negotiating and securing long-term contracts to generate hundreds of millions in transaction revenue.

Team Leadership: Mentor and manage a national team of Account Managers and Account Executives, fostering a high-performance culture and developing talent.

Strategic Account Development: Identify cross-selling and upselling opportunities within key accounts to maximize lifetime value and increase market share.

Sales & Marketing Strategy: Conceptualize and implement strategic sales and marketing programs to increase brand visibility and penetrate new market segments.

Stakeholder Management: Build and maintain strong relationships with C-suite client stakeholders to understand their business needs and drive long-term partnerships.

Required Qualifications:

Experience: 7+ years of experience in B2B sales, with a minimum of 3 years as a Key Account Manager or in a similar senior sales role within the oil, gas, or industrial sector.

Track Record: Proven success in consistently exceeding sales targets and generating significant new business revenue.

Leadership: Demonstrated experience in managing and developing a high-performing sales team.

Skills: Exceptional negotiation, communication, and strategic planning skills. Proficiency in client relationship management and pipeline management is essential.

Education: Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, or a related field is required. An advanced degree or relevant certification (e.g., CKAM) is a strong plus.

What We Offer:

Competitive base salary and a highly lucrative performance-based bonus structure.

Comprehensive benefits package.

Opportunity to lead strategic initiatives within a global organization.
