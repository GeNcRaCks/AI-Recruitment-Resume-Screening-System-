ob Title: Warehouse Operations Supervisor

Location: Los Angeles, CA

Company: InSync Logistics Solutions

Type: Full-time (Shift Work Required)

About the Role:
InSync Logistics Solutions, a leader in supply chain management, is looking for an experienced and hands-on Warehouse Operations Supervisor to lead our Los Angeles distribution center. You will be responsible for overseeing the daily workflow of our warehouse team, ensuring that all operations—from receiving to shipping—run safely, efficiently, and accurately. This is a critical role for maintaining high productivity and service levels for our partners.

Key Responsibilities:

Operational Oversight: Manage the receiving, warehousing, picking, packing, and shipping processes. Directly supervise a team of 20+ warehouse associates.

Performance & Productivity: Monitor KPIs to ensure high picking/packing accuracy and speed standards are consistently met. Implement process improvements to increase efficiency.

Inventory Management: Oversee inventory levels, conduct cycle counts, and ensure the accuracy of all records within the Warehouse Management System (WMS). Utilize ERP systems to track and manage materials.

Team Leadership: Lead, train, and motivate a diverse team, ensuring adherence to safety protocols and company policies. Manage workflow, schedules, and performance.

Quality Control: Ensure a clean and safe work environment. Implement loss prevention and inventory reduction strategies to minimize waste.

Required Qualifications:

Experience: Minimum of 4-6 years of progressive experience in a warehouse or distribution center environment, with at least 2 years in a supervisory or lead role.

Education: Associate's or Bachelor's degree in Logistics, Supply Chain Management, or a related field is highly preferred. Relevant certifications (e.g., Certified Forklift Driver) are a plus.

Technical Skills: Proficiency with WMS, ERP systems, and Microsoft Office Suite.

Hard Skills: Forklift certification and experience with inventory management techniques (e.g., Kanban). Knowledge of 5S or other lean methodologies is a plus.

Soft Skills: Strong leadership, problem-solving, and communication skills. Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, physically demanding environment.
